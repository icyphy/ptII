HierarchicalExecution

HierarchicalExecution is available as:

  • An applet.

This model shows that the Continuous director gives a correct execution of a hierarchical continuous-time model. By correct execution, we mean the simulation results (signals) of the hierarchical model are the same as the results of the same model with the hierarchy removed. The "signal 1" plotter plots a signal from the flat model, and the "signal 2" plotter plots a signal from the hierarchical model. The "difference" plotter plots the difference between these two signals.
