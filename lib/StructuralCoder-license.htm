StructuralCoder

"StructuralCoder is an engine that synthesizes controllers from formal specifications described under a subset of linear temporal logic (LTL). Importantly, it synthesizes synchronous dataflow controllers (in Lustre or in Ptolemy II form) and maintains requirement-to-code traceability. Such a feature is mandated in developing safety-critical systems and are considered essential for specification validation or integrating manual implementation such as legacy code. " StructuralCoder is developed by fortiss GmbH (An-Institut Technische Universität München) and is released under GNU LGPLv3.  See http://sourceforge.net/projects/structuralcoder/
